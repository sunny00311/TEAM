Offline AI Assistant

Privacy-First • Fully Offline • On-Device AI

Problem Statement

Most AI apps today depend on the internet and cloud servers, which can risk user privacy and stop working offline.

Our solution is an Android app that runs AI completely on the device using a local LLM and RAG pipeline. It stores documents locally, finds relevant information using vector embeddings, and generates answers without sending any data to the cloud.

This ensures privacy, fast responses, and full offline functionality.
